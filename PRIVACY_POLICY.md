# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 18, 2026

**Nodex** ("we", "us", or "our") operates the **SmartWallet** mobile application ("the App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

## Information Collection and Use

### Data We Collect

**Local Financial Data:**
- Daily transaction records (income and expenses)
- Transaction amounts and descriptions
- Financial balance information
- Profile settings and preferences

**Technical Data:**
- Device information (Android version, device model)
- App usage statistics (for crash reporting and improvements)

### How We Store Your Data

**Local Storage First:**
- All financial data is primarily stored locally on your device using Hive database
- Data remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to export or backup

**Optional Cloud Storage:**
- Users can optionally export data to cloud storage services
- We do not store your financial data on our servers

## Data Security

We prioritize your data security:
- All data is stored locally on your device
- No financial data is transmitted to our servers
- You control when and how to backup or export your data
- Easy data wipe option available in the app settings

## Permissions

The App requires the following permissions:

**Storage Permissions:**
- `MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE`, `WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE`, `READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE`
- Purpose: To export transaction history as PDF and backup data files
- You can deny these permissions, but PDF export and backup features will be unavailable

## Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that collect information:

- **Google Play Services:** For app distribution and crash analytics
- **Firebase (optional):** For crash reporting only

These services may collect:
- Device identifiers
- App performance data
- Crash logs

## Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.

## Your Rights

You have the following data protection rights:
- **Access:** View all your data stored in the app
- **Export:** Export your data as PDF or backup files
- **Deletion:** Permanently delete all data using the wipe feature
- **Control:** Choose whether to use cloud backup features
